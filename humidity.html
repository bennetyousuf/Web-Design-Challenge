Humidity

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg Latttitude). More interestingly, however, is that the Southern Hemisphere appears to be warmer thsi time of year than the Northern Hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the Earth at the time the data was prepared.
